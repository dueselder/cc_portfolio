About Me

With a decade-long career as a systems administrator, I have been at the forefront of numerous critical projects, ensuring seamless infrastructure management and robust system performance. Over the years, my passion for technology has only grown, but it was in the past year that I discovered a newfound love - programming.

Diving headfirst into the world of code, I've immersed myself in various technologies and languages, aiming to combine my foundational IT knowledge with modern web development practices. Here's a snapshot of my current skillset:

  • Web Basics: I have a solid understanding of HTML and CSS, being proficient in both. These are the building blocks of the web, and I'm confident in crafting structured and visually appealing web pages.
  • Scripting & Backend: While I'm still growing my expertise in JavaScript, PHP, and Python, I've undertaken projects that allowed me to get hands-on with these languages, creating dynamic functionalities and server-side operations.
  • Databases: My grasp on MySQL has enabled me to manage data effectively, ensuring that any application I work on has a robust backend support.
  • Version Control: I am well-acquainted with Git, leveraging its capabilities to maintain version histories efficiently. Additionally, my familiarity with GitHub allows me to collaborate with peers seamlessly, review code, and manage repositories.
  • Operating Systems: Having worked extensively on both Linux and Windows environments, I can navigate, troubleshoot, and optimize these systems with ease. My proficiency in Windows is further complemented by my skills in PowerShell, which aids in task automation and system management.

My journey from a systems administrator to delving into programming has been incredibly rewarding. I am eager to harness my accumulated skills, both old and new, to contribute to innovative projects and continue my learning journey in the ever-evolving realm of technology.
